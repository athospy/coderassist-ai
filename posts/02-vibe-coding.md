## Hook

Vibe coding will get you fired. Unless you understand what the AI wrote.

## Caption

"Vibe coding" is real and it works, until it doesn't.

The pattern is always the same: prompt the AI, paste the output, tweak until it runs, ship it. No architecture. No understanding. No tests.

What nobody says out loud is this: AI has lowered the bar for writing code while quietly raising the bar for engineering.

The skills that matter more now, not less:

→ System design — AI can't hold the full picture.
→ Code review — AI writes confidently incorrect code.
→ Debugging — someone has to find what AI broke.
→ Problem framing — garbage in, garbage out.

Speed is what AI gives you.
Judgment is what keeps you employed.

Which one are you investing in this week?
